# NIJA Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 27, 2026**

## Introduction

NIJA ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and trading platform services (the "Services").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect information that you provide directly to us:
- **Account Information**: Email address, username, password
- **Profile Information**: Name, phone number (optional)
- **Financial Information**: Trading API keys, exchange credentials (encrypted)
- **Payment Information**: Subscription payment details (processed by Stripe)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, trading activities, session duration
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, error logs
- **Location Data**: General location (for regulatory compliance)

### Trading Data
- **Trade History**: Executed trades, positions, profit/loss
- **Exchange Data**: API credentials (encrypted), account balances
- **Performance Metrics**: Win rate, trading statistics

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the NIJA trading platform
- Execute automated trades on your behalf
- Process subscription payments
- Send trading alerts and notifications
- Improve our Services and develop new features
- Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud
- Provide customer support
- Analyze platform performance and usage patterns

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

### Service Providers
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe (payment processing)
- **Cloud Hosting**: Railway, Heroku, or similar platforms
- **Analytics**: For app performance monitoring
- **Push Notifications**: Firebase Cloud Messaging

### Cryptocurrency Exchanges
- We transmit encrypted API credentials to exchanges you connect
- Trade execution data is shared with your chosen exchanges
- We never share your exchange credentials with third parties

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Prevent illegal activities or fraud

### Business Transfers
If NIJA is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption**: All API keys and sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit
- **TLS/SSL**: Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
- **Access Controls**: Restricted access to personal information
- **Secure Storage**: Encrypted database storage
- **Authentication**: JWT tokens with secure session management

### Your Responsibilities
- Maintain strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Secure your device with biometric authentication
- Never share your account credentials

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide Services:
- **Account Data**: Until account deletion
- **Trading Data**: 7 years (for regulatory compliance)
- **Financial Records**: 7 years (tax and regulatory requirements)
- **Log Data**: 90 days

You may request deletion of your account at any time through the app settings.

## Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### Access and Portability
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Download your trading history and data

### Correction
- Update or correct inaccurate information
- Modify account settings

### Deletion
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance

### Opt-Out
- Disable push notifications
- Unsubscribe from marketing emails
- Opt out of data collection (where applicable)

### California Residents (CCPA)
California residents have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination

### European Residents (GDPR)
EU/EEA residents have additional rights:
- Right to access, rectification, erasure
- Right to data portability
- Right to restrict or object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent
- Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers in compliance with applicable laws.

## Third-Party Links
