## üí° Study Tips

### 1. Practice-First Learning
Don't just read - actually do the exercises. Hands-on experience is crucial.

### 2. Start with Paper Trading
Never risk real money until you've completed at least Lessons 1-16 and tested extensively.

### 3. One Lesson at a Time
Don't rush. Master each concept before moving forward.

### 4. Keep Notes
Document your configuration changes, test results, and observations.

### 5. Join the Community
Connect with other NIJA traders to share experiences and learn faster.

### 6. Regular Review
Revisit earlier lessons as you progress - you'll understand them better with experience.

### 7. Test Everything
Never deploy configuration changes without testing in paper trading first.
