### 3. Full Regulatory Compliance for Managed Capital / Fund Operations

**Current State:**
- Technical risk management systems
- Internal governance and version control
- Strategy documentation

**What's Required:**

#### Legal Structure
- **Registered Investment Advisor (RIA)** or equivalent
- **Fund structure** (LP, LLC, offshore fund, etc.)
- **Compliance officer** (designated and registered)
- **Legal counsel** specializing in investment management
- **Fund administrator** for independent NAV calculation

#### Regulatory Registration
- **SEC Registration** (for US-based operations)
  - Form ADV filing
  - State registrations if required
  - FINRA registration if applicable
- **CFTC Registration** (if trading futures/commodities)
  - CPO (Commodity Pool Operator) registration
  - CTA (Commodity Trading Advisor) registration
- **International registrations** if operating globally

#### Compliance Requirements
- **AML/KYC procedures** (Anti-Money Laundering / Know Your Customer)
- **Best execution policies**
- **Code of Ethics** and Personal Trading Policy
- **Privacy policies** (GDPR, CCPA compliance)
- **Cybersecurity policies** and incident response plan
- **Business continuity plan**
- **Disaster recovery procedures**

#### Reporting & Documentation
- **Form PF** (for larger funds, SEC requirement)
- **Form CPO-PQR** (CFTC requirement)
- **Quarterly investor letters**
- **Annual audited financial statements**
- **Performance attribution reports**
- **Risk reports** to investors
- **Compliance manual** (100+ pages typical)

#### Investor Protection
- **Offering memorandum** / Private Placement Memorandum (PPM)
- **Limited Partnership Agreement** (LPA) or Operating Agreement
- **Subscription agreements**
- **Risk disclosures** (comprehensive)
- **Conflicts of interest** disclosure
- **Fee transparency** documentation

**Professionals Required:**
- **Compliance Officer** (full-time or outsourced)
- **Legal Counsel** (securities attorney)
- **Accountant/Auditor** (fund accounting specialist)
- **Fund Administrator** (independent third-party)
- **Tax Advisor** (fund taxation specialist)

**Costs:**
- Legal setup: $50,000 - $200,000
- Annual compliance: $100,000 - $500,000
- Fund administration: $50,000 - $200,000/year
- Audit: $25,000 - $100,000/year
- Registration fees: $5,000 - $25,000/year
- Insurance (E&O, D&O): $25,000 - $100,000/year

**Total Initial Cost:** $250,000 - $750,000
**Total Annual Operating Cost:** $200,000 - $1,000,000

**Timeline:**
- Legal structure: 2-3 months
- Regulatory registration: 3-6 months
- First investor ready: 6-12 months
